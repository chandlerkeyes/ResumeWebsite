About Me

Content Creator

Ever since the 8th grade, I had a passion for creating content that could educate and inspire others. I started off with my cell phone camera and and a free video editing software, windows movie maker. I uploaded videos like makeup tutorials or funny skits that got little views. As time when by, I was noticed by brands and people outside of my location. My channel grew like wild fire in the 10th grade. Now, I've made a career out of it. Creating a YouTube channel helped me discover what my true purpose is. To develop and deploy art that everyone can experience.

What are my goals?

I always go by the quote, "knowlege is power". My goal is to benefit who I am working with. In my eyes, the more you know, the more useful you are. Through my journey as a software developer, I aspire to become a fullstack developer and use my YouTube channel to spread the knowlege for people who were once curious like myself.

Coding is Life

Who knew a small coffee date would have lead me into the coding world. While attending Eastern Michigan Univsersity with a Film major, I decided to test the waters and take an Intro to Programming course. This led me to the passion of becoming a Java Developer. Soon after, I decided to enroll in Grand Circus's Java Bootcamp. We were taught the wonders of back-end coding and a little bit of HTML and CSS. I got a feel for what it's like to work in an agile environment as well as with a team of developers with different skillsets. Currently, I am a Front-End Intern at YAC Social in Grand Rapids, striving to learn both front and back end. Soon enough, I will be fullstack.

Projects

Pickup Time Application

If you like using websites like meetup.com then you would love Pickup Time. This application was made for athletes who want to play games right away with people in their area. The user is prompted to enter a location, day, time, and the amount of people required to attend this event. Other users will have the ability to add themselves to an event. Once an event reaches the required number of people. Our Twillio API will send a notifcation directly to your phone, confirming that the event is happening.

Library Terminal

This simple console application allows you to keep track of your checked out books. When you run the program, you are provided with a list of books, given the option to return/checkout your book of choice. The application will then generate a due date which is set to two weeks from checking it out.

Sales Tax

Nothing gets more convieniant than a tax calculator. This application allows you to enter any item you're intereseted in purchasing and will output your subtotal, tax, and total price.

Experience

The Java Bootcamp is a 10 week program with 8 hours of intense Java training Monday through Friday. This full time bootcamp requires the willingness to learn, and being able to tolerate constructive criticism. The bootcamp helped us build our team work, problem solving, and communcation skills. We were also taught languages outside of core Java such as HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
